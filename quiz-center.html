Question 1
According to the Second Law of Thermodynamics, which statement is correct?
A
Energy can be created in an isolated system
B
The entropy of an isolated system never decreases
C
Heat flows from cold to hot bodies spontaneously
D
All processes are reversible given enough energy
✦ Explanation
The Second Law states that in any thermodynamic process, the total entropy of an isolated system tends to increase over time. This means energy spontaneously disperses — heat flows from hot to cold, not the other way around. Option B correctly captures this fundamental principle.
